# Narrative Arc: The Descent (9 Sectors)

**META**
- **ID**: `narrative-arc`
- **STATUS**: `DRAFT`
- **THEME**: "The Inversion" - Descent from Physical Decay to Metaphysical Singularity.

---

## Act I: The Crust (Physical Decay)

### Sector 9: The Rust Sprawl (Start)
*   **Concept**: The forgotten surface. Massive heaps of scrap metal, constant ash rain from the burning atmosphere.
*   **Theme**: `INDUSTRIAL` (Brown/Rust/Grey).
*   **Key Faction**: **Scavengers (Free Archons)**.
*   **Boss**: *The Gatekeeper* (Heavy Mech).
*   **Narrative**: Establish the hopelessness of the surface. Unlock the gate to the underworld.

### Sector 8: The Arteries (Logistics)
*   **Concept**: A massive automated transit hub that never stops. Mazes of shipping containers and maglev rails.
*   **Theme**: `INDUSTRIAL` (Yellow hazard stripes, Concrete).
*   **Key Faction**: **Vanguard Remnant** (Automated Security).
*   **Narrative**: The machinery of the old world runs without purpose.

### Sector 7: The Hive (Residential)
*   **Concept**: Densely packed coffin-apartments and neon markets. The last place humans lived before the end.
*   **Theme**: `RESIDENTIAL` (Neon Blue/Pink, Dark Blue shadows).
*   **Key Faction**: **The Convergence** (Recruiting the desperate).
*   **Narrative**: Witness the voluntary upload of humanity into the Shadow.

---

## Act II: The Organs (System Function)

### Sector 6: The Green Lung (Hydroponics)
*   **Concept**: Life support systems gone wrong. Genetically modified flora has overtaken the machinery.
*   **Theme**: `ORGANIC` (Toxic Green, Mud, Bioluminescence).
*   **Key Faction**: **Empyreal Council** (Nature reclaiming tech).
*   **Narrative**: Biological life is messy and uncontrollable.

### Sector 5: The Iron Heart (Foundry)
*   **Concept**: Geothermal power and manufacturing. Rivers of molten slag. Extreme heat.
*   **Theme**: `INDUSTRIAL` (Orange/Red/Black).
*   **Key Faction**: **Vanguard Remnant** (Elite Guards).
*   **Narrative**: The Vanguards power source. Breaking this weakens their grip.

### Sector 4: The Memory Banks (Archives)
*   **Concept**: Endless rows of servers kept at absolute zero. The digitized souls of the uploaded.
*   **Theme**: `HIGH_TECH` (Ice Blue, White, Chrome).
*   **Key Faction**: **The Convergence** (Protecting the uploaded).
*   **Narrative**: The realization that "The Cloud" is just a cold tomb.

---

## Act III: The Soul (Metaphysical Breakdown)

### Sector 3: Chimera Labs (Research)
*   **Concept**: Where Project SYMBIOSIS began. Horrific experiments merging flesh and machine.
*   **Theme**: `CLINICAL` (White tiles, Blood Red stains).
*   **Key Faction**: **Corrupted** (Failed experiments).
*   **Narrative**: The unethical origins of the player character.

### Sector 2: The Black Gate (Containment)
*   **Concept**: The final physical barrier. Physics begins to break. Gravity is weird.
*   **Theme**: `VOID` (Deep Purple, Obsidian, Stars).
*   **Key Faction**: **Vanguard Remnant** (Last Stand).
*   **Narrative**: The Vanguard trying to hold back the inevitable.

### Sector 1: Event Horizon
*   **Concept**: Reality glitch. Wall textures load wrong. Enemies are wireframes.
*   **Theme**: `ABSTRACT` (Glitch artifacts, RGB separation).
*   **Narrative**: The simulation is crashing.

### Sector 0: The Core
*   **Concept**: The Singularity.
*   **Objective**: Final Choice (Contain, Merge, or Resolve).
