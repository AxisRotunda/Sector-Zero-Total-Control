## 2. Common Slang & Sayings
* **"Glitched"**: Crazy, unstable, or corrupted by psionics. *"Stay away from him, he's glitched."*
* **"Proxy"**: Fake, bullshit, a lie. *"That contract is pure Proxy."*
* **"Zeroed"**: Dead, killed. *"He got zeroed by a sniper."*
* **"The Hum"**: The sound of psionic resonance. *"Can you hear the Hum?"*
* **"Brick"**: Non-ARD human (derogatory). *"Just another brick in the wall."*
